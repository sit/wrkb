# Aurora

## Roles

Mid Lane

## Base Stats

- Health: 630 (+89 per level)
- Health Reg. (5s): 8 (+1 per level)
- Mana: 420 (+37 per level)
- Mana Reg. (5s): 14 (+1 per level)
- Armor: 34 (+4 per level)
- Magic Res.: 36 (+2 per level)
- Move Speed: 350 (+0 per level)
- Attack Dmg.: 54 (+3 per level)
- Attack Spd.: 0.75 (+0.0136 per level)

## Abilities

### [P] Spirit Abjuration

Damaging an enemy 3 times with abilities or attacks exorcises them, dealing 2.5% ( +2%AP ) of the target's max Health as magic damage . If the enemy is a champion, it also frees a spirit that follows Aurora for 4 seconds, restoring her Health by 1 - 18 ( +2% AP ) every second ( max 4 spirits) .

### [1] Twofold Hex

**Cooldown:** 8.0 / 7.0 / 7.0 / 6.0 seconds

**Cost:** 60.0 / 60.0 / 60.0 / 60.0

First Cast : Fires cursed energy ion a direction, dealing 40 / 70 / 100 / 130 ( +32% AP ) magic damage to enemies and cursing them for 3.5 seconds. Recast : Detonates the curse, dealing up to 45 / 75 / 105 / 135 ( +32% AP ) magic damage to enemies passed through. Enemies take up to 50% bonus damage based on missing Health . Damage beyond the first hit is reduced by 20% . Deals 40% damage against minions and monsters instead. If the curse's duration runs out, Aurora will automatically recast the ability.

### [2] Across the Veil

**Cooldown:** 21.0 / 20.0 / 19.0 / 18.0 seconds

**Cost:** 80.0 / 80.0 / 80.0 / 80.0

Hops in a direction. Upon landing, enters the spirit realm, becoming invisible for 1 / 1.2 / 1.4 / 1.6 seconds and entering Realm Hopper . Real Hopper : Gains 25% / 30% / 35% / 40% Movement Speed for 4 seconds. Scoring a takedown on an enemy champion resets this ability's cooldown.

### [3] The Weirding

**Cooldown:** 13.0 / 12.0 / 11.0 / 10.0 seconds

**Cost:** 80.0 / 80.0 / 80.0 / 80.0

Converges the realms, sending out a blast of spirit magic that deals 75 / 125 / 175 / 225 ( +65% AP ) magic damage to enemies in the area and slows them by 80% , decaying over 1 second. Aurora hops backward a short distance after casting.

### [4] Between Worlds

**Cooldown:** 100.0 / 90.0 / 80.0 seconds

**Cost:** 100.0 / 100.0 / 100.0

First Cast : Leaps in a direction. Upon landing, converges the realms, sending out a pulse of spirit energy that deals 150 / 250 / 350 ( +50% AP ) magic damage and slows enemies hit by 30% for 2 seconds. The area of convergence stays active for 2.5 / 3.25 / 4 seconds, granting Aurora Realm Hopper and allowing her to jump from one edge of the area to another. Enemies attempting to enter or leave the area are slowed by 50% for 1.5 / 1.75 / 2 seconds. Recast : Ends the area of convergence effect early.
