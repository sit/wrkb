## Abilities

### [P] Dragon Practice

Dragon Practice : Hitting enemy champions with abilities and killing enemies with Super Scorcher Breath grant a stack of Dragon Practice, which improves Smolder's basic abilities. Dragon Practice Empowering Effects : - Super Scorcher Breath : Deals an additional 40% magic damage and gains new effects at 20, 85 and 150 stacks. - Achooo! : Explosions deal an additional 55% magic damage . - Flap, Flap, Flap : Each hit deals an additional 12% magic damage and gains an extra bolt ever 65 stacks.

### [1] Super Scorcher Breath

**Cooldown:** 5.0 / 5.0 / 4.0 / 4.0 seconds

**Cost:** 30.0 / 30.0 / 30.0 / 30.0

Belches flame, dealing 60 / 95 / 130 / 165 ( +110% bonus AD ) physical damage and 40% magical damage . If the target dies, 15 Mana is refunded, once per cast. Dragon Practice Empowering Effects : -20 stacks: Damages all enemies nearby the target. -85 stacks: Sends 2 explosions beyond the target that deal 50% of this ability's damage. -150 stacks: Burns the target, dealing 0% ( +0.025% bonus AD ) of their max Health as true damage over 3 seconds. Enemy champions that drop below 6.5% of their max HP while burning are killed instantly. This ability's damage scales with Critical Rate, increased by 7.5% damage per 10% Crit Rate (max 75% ).

### [2] Achooo!

**Cooldown:** 12.0 / 11.0 / 10.0 / 9.0 seconds

**Cost:** 50.0 / 55.0 / 60.0 / 65.0

Lets out a flaming sneeze, dealing 65 / 85 / 105 / 125 ( +60% bonus AD ) physical damage and slowing by 35% for 1.5 seconds. Sneeze Blast : Hitting champions causes an explosion, dealing 10 / 35 / 60 / 85 ( +55% bonus AD +80% AP ) physical damage and 55% magic damage in an area. Each consecutive explosion does 75% damage . Smolder gains 1 Dragon Practice stack per champion hit. Deals 130% damage against minions and monsters.

### [3] Flap, Flap, Flap

**Cooldown:** 18.0 / 16.0 / 14.0 / 12.0 seconds

**Cost:** 65.0 / 65.0 / 65.0 / 65.0

Gains 75% Movement Speed and ignores terrain for 1.25 seconds. Boom! : While flying, automatically locks on to the lowest Health enemy in range and bombards them 5 times. Each hit deals 15 / 20 / 25 / 30 ( +30% AD ) physical damage and 12% magic damage . Dragon Vision : Gains expanded vision while flying. Prioritizes champions for bombarding.

### [4] MMOOOMMMM!

**Cooldown:** 80.0 / 70.0 / 60.0 seconds

**Cost:** 100.0 / 100.0 / 100.0

Smolder's mom breathes fire from above, dealing 200 / 300 / 400 ( +110% bonus AD +100% AP ) physical damage to enemies in range. Enemies in the center take 300 / 450 / 600 ( +165% bonus AD +150% AP ) physical damage and are slowed by 40% for 2 seconds. Smolder heals for 100 / 135 / 170 ( +50% bonus AD +75% AP ) Health if he is hit. Deals 50% damage against minions and monsters.
