# Yuumi

Support

- Health Reg. (5s): 11 (+1 per level)
- Mana: 435 (+37 per level)
- Mana Reg. (5s): 15 (+1 per level)
- Armor: 40 (+3 per level)
- Magic Res.: 30 (+1 per level)
- Move Speed: 340 (+0 per level)
- Attack Dmg.: 50 (+3 per level)
- Attack Spd.: 0.80 (+0.0043 per level)

## Abilities

### [P] Feline Friendship

Passive : When Yuumi's attacks and abilities strike champions, she heals herself for 67 ( +25% AP ) Health . If Yuumi Attaches to an allied champion within 4 seconds, they are healed for the same amount as well. (18 second cooldown) Bonds of Friendship : While Attached , Yuumi gains Friendship with her ally whenever they kill a champion or minion. The ally with the most Friendship becomes her Best Fried . Best Friend Bonus : Yuumi's abilities gain bonus effects while she is Attached to her Best Friend .

### [1] Prowling Projectile

**Cooldown:** 5.0 / 5.0 / 5.0 / 5.0 seconds

**Cost:** 60.0 / 60.0 / 60.0 / 60.0

Missile Summon : Hold and drag the ability button to steer the missile. Base Effect : Deals 60 / 100 / 140 / 180 / 220 ( +20% AP ) magic damage . Attached Bonus : If the missile takes at least 1 second to get to its target, its damage increases to 100 / 160 / 220 / 280 / 340 ( +35% AP ) magic damage . It also slows by 45% / 50% / 55% / 60% / 65% and reveals the target for 1 second. Best Friend Bonus : The missile always slows the target hit. Hitting a champion with the missile grants the ally Yuumi is Attached to 18 / 19 / 20 / 21 / 22 ( +5% AP ) magic damage on-hit for 5 seconds.

### [2] You and Me!

**Cooldown:** 10.0 / 10.0 / 10.0 / 10.0 seconds

Best Friend Bonus (Passive) : While on her Best Friend , Yuumi gains an additional 6% / 7% / 8% / 9% ( +0.02% AP ) Heal and Shield Power . Attach (Active) : Dashes to an allied champion and Attaches to them. While Attached , she follows her partner's movement and is untargetable except by turrets. Immobilizing effects on Yuumi place this ability on a 5 seconds cooldown.

### [3] Zoomies

**Cooldown:** 10.0 / 10.0 / 10.0 / 10.0 seconds

**Cost:** 80.0 / 90.0 / 100.0 / 110.0

Gains a shield that absorbs 85 / 110 / 135 / 160 ( +30% AP ) damage and 24% / 28% / 32% / 36% ( +8% AP ) Attack Speed for 3 seconds. While the shield persists, she also gains 20% Movement Speed . If Yuumi is Attached , this ability affects her ally instead.

### [4] Final Chapter

**Cooldown:** 85.0 / 75.0 / 65.0 seconds

**Cost:** 100.0 / 100.0 / 100.0

Channels for 3.5 seconds, launching 7 magical waves : While Attached , hold and drag the ability button to steer the waves. Against enemies (per wave) : Deals 80 / 100 / 120 ( +15% AP ) magic damage . Slows by 10% for 1.25 seconds, increased by 10% per wave hit. On allied champions (per wave ): Restores 20 / 30 / 4 0 ( +5% AP ) Health . Excess healing is converted into a shield instead. Best Friend Bonus : For her Best Friend , the heal is increased to 26 / 39 / 52 ( +8% AP ) Health . Wave Damage Stacks : Waves after the first deal 20 / 30 / 40 ( +5% AP ) magic damage . Yuumi can move and cast You and Me! and Zoomies while channeling.
