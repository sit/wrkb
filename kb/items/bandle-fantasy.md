# Bandle Fantasy

**Type:** Magic  
**Cost:** 3000 gold

## Stats

- +300 Mana
- +85 Ability Power
- +20 Ability Haste
- +7 Magic Penetration

## Effects

FLAMEBLOSSOM: Damaging an enemy champion with an active ability spawns a trap at their feet. After a brief delay, the trap deals magic damage equal to 105 plus 15% Ability Power to enemies in it (8s cooldown per target). If a target is hit by more than one trap set by the same champion within a short duration, traps after the first deal only 20% damage.
