# Essence Reaver

**Type:** Physical  
**Cost:** 3000 gold

## Stats

- +35 Attack Damage
- +25% Critical Strike Chance
- +20 Ability Haste

## Effects

UNIQUE - Mana Siphon: Attacks restore 3% missing Mana on-hit.

UNIQUE - Spellblade: Casting an ability generates a Spellblade charge (max 3 charges) that lasts up to 10s. Hitting an enemy with an attack consumes a charge, dealing 90% base Attack Damage as physical damage and granting 40 Movement Speed for 2s, This Bonus damage can Critically Strike. Each ability generates only one charge per 2s.
