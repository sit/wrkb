# Glorious

**Type:** Enchantment  
**Cost:** 500 gold

## Effects

UNIQUE - GLORY: Gain 30% bonus movement speed for 4 seconds, increasing to 75% towards enemy champions or turrets. After 4 seconds or upon reaching an enemy champion emit a shockwave that slows enemy champions in range by 60% for 2s and grants 20% Tenacity and 20% Slow Resist for 3s.

Limited to 1 Boots enchantment.
