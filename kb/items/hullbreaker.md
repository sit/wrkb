# Hullbreaker

**Type:** Physical  
**Cost:** 3100 gold

## Stats

- +400 Health
- +50 Attack Damage

## Effects

UNIQUE - SET SAIL: +5% Movement Speed.

UNIQUE - BOARDING PARTY: Nearby allied siege and super minions gain 20~130 Armor and 10~120 Magic Resist (25% if you are a ranged champion)

UNIQUE - SKIPPER: Every 4th attack against champions and epic monsters deals bonus physical damage equal to 160% base Attack Damage + 5% max Health (ranged champions deal 40% of the damage), increased to 240% Attack Damage + 9% Max Health Bonus as Physical Damage against structures (ranged champions deal 40% of the damage).
