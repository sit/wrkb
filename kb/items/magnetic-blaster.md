# Magnetic Blaster

**Type:** Physical  
**Cost:** 3000 gold

## Stats

- +25 Attack Damage
- +25% Attack Speed
- +25% Critical Strike Chance

## Effects

Fervor: +5% Movement Speed.

Energized: Moving and attacking will generate an Energized Attack.

Power Blitz:  Energized Attacks gain 100 range (50 range for melee attacks), deal 40-100 bonus magic damage, and grant 60 Movement Speed for 0.75s. This Damage bounces up to 5 enemies and can Critically Strike. (Deals 50% - 80% damage against minions.)
