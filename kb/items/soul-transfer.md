# Soul Transfer

**Type:** Physical  
**Cost:** 3200 gold

## Stats

- +25 Attack Damage
- +30% Attack Speed
- +25% Critical Strike Chance

## Effects

UNIQUE - SHADOW DANCE: When your attack Critically Strikes an enemy champion or an epic monster, summon a clone that lasts 4s to attack nearby enemies, the clone inherits 20% of your Attack Damage and additionally gains 30% of your Critical Rate as Attack speed. Up to two clones can exist at one time. When your attack Critically Strikes an enemy champion or an epic monster, summon a clone that lasts 4s to attack nearby enemies, the clone inherits 25% of your Attack Damage and additionally gains 30% of your Crit. If a clone moves more than 600 units away from you, it will disappear early.
