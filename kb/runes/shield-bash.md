# Shield Bash

**Type:** Minor
**Family:** Domination

## Description

After receiving a healing or shielding, your next attack against an enemy champion will be empowered, dealing bonus 15-50 + 15% bonus Attack Damage + 10% Adaptive Ability Power (7s cooldown).
