# Sudden Impact

**Type:** Minor
**Family:** Domination

## Description

For 4 seconds after exiting stealth or using a dash, leap, blink, or teleport, damaging an enemy champion additionally deals 10 + 5 * (character level - 1) true damage. (10s cooldown)
